Pasta: water, oil, pasta sticks, vegetables, sauces

Description

Pasta is a versatile and popular Italian dish made from durum wheat semolina or flour, water, and sometimes eggs. It comes in various shapes and sizes, such as spaghetti, penne, and fettuccine, offering a wide range of textures. Pasta can be boiled and served with an array of sauces, from simple tomato and basil to creamy Alfredo or pesto. It serves as a staple in many cuisines worldwide, appreciated for its ease of preparation, adaptability, and ability to pair with diverse ingredients, making it a comfort food enjoyed globally.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of your favorite pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for some heat)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Steps

  1. Boil the Pasta
  2. Prepare the Sauce
  3. Cook the Tomatoes
  4. Combine Pasta and Sauce
  5. Add Fresh Basil
  6. Serve
